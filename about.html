About Us

Like most businesses in India, our story too begins with the family. Way back in the 1950’s with the nation basking in the zeal of post-independence, life and lifestyles were very different from today’s gadget and gizmo world. But for the enterprising and bold, no barrier can be insurmountable, no mountain too high to conquer. The Echjay family dazzled with dynamism as it made its foray into the colourful and challenging world of manufacturing textiles. From manufacturing units in metal forgings to surgical gloves and electronic capacitors, the Echjay Group did it all. Expansion plans nudged it towards real estate, across generations and today the beacon proudly shines in a niche category, Redevelopment, where trust is paramount and speed defines the crucial make or break parameter of every project.
