Required Documents for the Rejuvenating Process called Redevelopment

Redevelopment is no longer tricky when you have the experts by your side to safeguard your interests, with correct procedures and legalities. Legal documents that are a must-follow, must-do.

  • Property Card
  • Property Tax Bill
  • Water Tax Bill
  • Original Purchase Document
  • Individual Agreement of Owners (for housing societies and shop owners)
  • Existing Area Documents
  • Legal Documents pertaining to the land or property

Additional Documents Reviewed by Echjay

There is nothing like too much caution and we at Echjay go that extra mile to scrutinise the following documents as a necessary part of every redevelopment project:

  • Road Widening Documents
  • Road Line Remark Documents
  • Inspection Extract
  • Copy of C.S. Plan
  • Registration Stamped Copy
  • Copy of D.P. Remark
